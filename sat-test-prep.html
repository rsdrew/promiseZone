Tackle the SAT with the help of the Promise Zone!

The Hazel Park Promise Zone is proud to offer SAT testing preparation for all 11th grade students at Hazel Park High School. We use the curriculum from College Spring to ensure that our students are fully prepared for the SAT. This test is a major factor in determining where students attend college, so it’s essential we prepare them to the fullest.

See the difference of a higher SAT score

More financial aid

Most colleges offer financial aid based on a combination of your GPA and SAT scores. A higher SAT score means less money out of your pockets.

Increased options

With a higher SAT score, you’ll have a wider selection of colleges to choose from. This will help you pick the best-fitting college for you.

Better course placement

Some colleges use SAT scores to determine course placement, allowing students with higher scores to skip introductory courses and enroll in more advanced classes.
