Support our mission with your donation!

The mentoring program pairs students with mentors to provide resources and encouragement from high school graduation through the first year of college. The program engages alumni, community volunteers, and others to support the Promise Zone's goals.

The Promise Zone aims to ensure college-bound students have trusted sources to turn to when issues arise. This program is open to all Hazel Park graduates, with priority given to those planning to enroll in community college.

Students will...

Receive up to $300 in additional scholarships

Meet with their mentor in the Spring before graduation

Discuss goals and objectives for their first year in college

Check in with their mentor every month to stay on track

Bring any questions, comments, or concerns to their mentor for immediate guidance

Mentors will...

Receive up to $300 in scholarships if enrolled in college

Guide their student through their first year of college

Attend an orientation session with training in the Spring

Receive talking points and resource guides

Connect with their student at least once a month
