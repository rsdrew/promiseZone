Scholarships are a "last-dollar scholarship", meaning students will draw from other funding sources before being awarded the Promise Zone Scholarship.

For example, a student who is eligible for $1,500 of the Promise Zone scholarship and receives $1,000 from a Pell Grant will only receive $500 from the Promise Zone.
