Why choose the Hazel Park Promise Zone?

The Hazel Park Promise and College Access Network is committed to revitalizing the Hazel Park community by increasing its educational attainment and making it an attractive destination for families. We do this by offering a tuition-free path to an associate's degree to resident graduates of Hazel Park Schools and by recruiting and providing college and career preparatory programs to support Hazel Park students.

Students face various challenges when preparing for college, including navigating the complexities of FAFSA applications, selecting the right college amidst numerous options, and performing well on standardized tests like the SAT. Additionally, finding sufficient financial aid to cover tuition and other expenses adds to their stress. Without personalized guidance, students often feel overwhelmed and uncertain about their future paths.

The Hazel Park Promise Zone provides a holistic approach to address these challenges. Our dedicated team offers personalized assistance with FAFSA applications, expert guidance in navigating college selection, extensive preparation for the SAT, and a diverse catalog of scholarships. Through our mentorship program and ongoing support, students receive comprehensive guidance, encouragement, and resources to not only succeed in college but also to flourish in their academic and professional pursuits, ensuring a brighter future for themselves and their communities.
